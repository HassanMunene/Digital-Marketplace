Welcome to Benolives, your trusted partner in cutting-edge software solutions.

Our Journey.

Benolives was founded in 2012 by Kelvin Gitau , who recognized the growing need for robust cybersecurity solutions in Africa. Starting as a small team, we aimed to provide innovative security software that would protect businesses from the ever-evolving landscape of cyber threats.

Over the years, we have expanded our team, partnered with leading security providers, and completed numerous projects, all while staying true to our mission of making technology accessible and secure for everyone. Today, we are proud to serve over 200 clients, helping them navigate their digital transformations with confidence.
